

## Security Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2, 2026

Welcome to our website. We take the security of our platform, users, content creators, and visitors seriously. This Security Policy explains the measures we use to protect our website, YouTube-related content, user information, and digital services from unauthorized access, misuse, and cyber threats.

---

# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to:

* Protect user accounts and personal information
* Prevent unauthorized access to the website
* Secure video content and digital assets
* Reduce risks from hacking, malware, spam, and phishing
* Ensure safe interaction between users and the platform

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* Website administrators
* Developers and moderators
* Registered users and visitors
* Third-party integrations and APIs
* All devices accessing the platform

---

# 3. Account Security

To maintain account security:

* Strong passwords are required for all admin accounts
* Passwords should contain:

  * Uppercase letters
  * Lowercase letters
  * Numbers
  * Special characters
* Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is strongly recommended
* Login attempts may be rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks
* Suspicious login activity may trigger temporary account suspension

---

# 4. Data Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data, including:

* SSL/TLS encryption for website traffic
* Secure password hashing
* Protected databases and server access controls
* Regular software and security updates
* Limited administrative access

Sensitive information is never intentionally sold or shared without consent unless required by law.

---

# 5. Content Protection

To protect videos, graphics, branding, and intellectual property:

* Unauthorized copying or redistribution of content is prohibited
* Automated scraping or downloading tools are not allowed
* Copyright infringement may result in legal action or bans

---

# 6. Acceptable Use

Users must not:

* Attempt unauthorized access to the website
* Upload malware, harmful scripts, or malicious code
* Use bots for spam or abuse
* Conduct phishing, fraud, or impersonation
* Exploit vulnerabilities for personal gain

Violations may lead to account suspension, IP bans, or legal reporting.

---

# 7. Third-Party Services

Our website may integrate with third-party platforms such as:

* YouTube APIs
* Google services
* Analytics providers
* Payment processors
* Advertising networks

While we strive to work with trusted providers, we are not responsible for the independent security practices of third-party services.

---

# 8. Security Monitoring

We may monitor:

* Login activity
* Traffic anomalies
* Suspicious behavior
* Failed authentication attempts
* Potential abuse patterns

This helps us detect and prevent security threats.
